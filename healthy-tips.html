DIET TIP OF THE DAY- GAINING/LOOSING WEIGHT ON A BUDGET!!!!

 Peanut butter is high in calories and fat at 188 calories and 16 grams of fat (good fat with omega3,6 fatty acids) per two tablespoon serving. It is also a good source of protein at 8 grams per two tablespoon serving. It is best to use all natural peanut butter that will cost you approx $4-$5. Throw it on some whole grain wheat bread which is high in complex carbs so you have long lasting energy for your day for approx. $2-$3 a loaf. A single large hard-boiled egg contains about 6.5 grams of protein and they only cost approx. $1.75 a dozen. Since eggs are fast-digesting forms of protein, eat them in a post-workout meal as well! I personally eat this for breakfast allmost every morning! For only $8, you have a meal that will last you 2weeks...WOW! For people trying to loose weight, you may want to buy low calorie whole grain bread or just eat 1 slice of bread with peanut butter on it

Oatmeal for Breakfast

Add oatmeal to your breakfast smootie/shake or add fruit to your bowl of oatmeal. Oatmeal is a complex carbohydrate that will give you long lasting energy for your day and plus its good for the heart!

Summer Time Snack!

Cut up fresh fruit into bite size pieces. Put them into a freezer safe container into your freezer. Take them out when they are frozen and enjoy! Suck on them like hard candy. This will prevent you from eating over your portion and there good to eat in the summer time

Salads

All salads aren't good salads. Too much cheese, croutons, and dressing can kill a healthy salad. Try eating no croutons, a sprinkle of cheese, and 2tsp of vinaigrette dressing!

Watch Your Diet

 Following a careful diet is one of the basic rules of weight loss. A careful diet includes:

 Eating food low in fat and low in calories

 Avoiding processed food

 Prefer barbecued food and stay away from fried food

 Prefer fish and white meat instead of red meat

 Drink 8 glasses of water per day

 Eat fruits, vegetables and low fat dairy products

 Do not skip breakfast

 Eat smaller meals every 3-4 hours

 Eat slowly and chew your food well

 No junk food or sugar loaded drinks

 Eat your last meal of the day at least 3 hours before sleeping

 Eat salads with no dressings

 You need to understand that when it comes to losing weight that here are no fast ways to achieve the results you want. Stay away from crash diets or celebrity diets and do not trust weight loss programs or pills. Always look for healthy ways to lose weight and be very selective of who to trust. If you can afford it you can also get professional advice from a certified nutritionist who can help you build up a diet and is suited for you. Stay committed to your goals and it is only a matter of time to get the results you want .

 

Drink Water

Water is one of the best appetite suppressants known. A fast and easy way to start losing weight is by drinking a glass of water before each meal. The added volume will fill up your stomach, causing you to eat less, and consume fewer calories. If you want to lose weight faster then you need to drink water.

GET TESTED

Many people suggest things to take to lose weight or get healthier. One big example is taking a multi vitamin every day. The Only way you know if you even need these things is if you go get a medical physical with a blood test. It will show what is missing out your diet so you’re not adding things in your body you don't need.

Fat-free is not a license to overeat!

For the past 30 years, we have been told to cut the fat to lose weight. People filled their shopping carts with lowfat or fat-free foods and ate to their stomachs' content. But as many gained weight instead of losing it, people began to wonder what went wrong. Firstly, lowfat products are made by replacing the fat with sugar, making them low in fat, but high in calories. Secondly, many lowfat foods are filled with refined carbohydrates, which cause the body to oversecrete insulin and promote the storage of fat. Your best bet is to choose foods that are naturally low in fat and calories, such as fruits and vegetables, and to include a balance of complex carbohydrates, lean proteins and healthy fats at each meal.
